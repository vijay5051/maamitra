## Full description (4000 char max — used ~2,400)

```
MaaMitra is your AI-powered mitra for every step of Indian motherhood — from the first positive test to your child's early years. Built for Indian mothers, in the way a trusted friend would actually talk to you.

WHY MOTHERS CHOOSE MAAMITRA

• Ask anything, anytime — fevers, feeding, sleep, milestones, government schemes. Your mitra is awake at 2 a.m.
• India-first by design — Indian foods, cultural context, multiple Indian states and schemes built in. Not a Western app with Indian labels stuck on.
• Remembers you and your baby — your mitra knows your baby's age, your pregnancy stage, your family situation, and picks up where you left off.
• Information you can trust — content written with Indian Academy of Pediatrics (IAP) and FOGSI guidelines as reference, so you know what you're reading lines up with what Indian doctors actually follow.
• A private parent community — read, post, and connect with Indian mothers going through the same stage as you. Moderated, respectful, ad-free.

WHAT'S INSIDE

AI chat — a warm, informed companion for every parenting question, in plain Hindi-English your mitra actually speaks.

Growth & milestones — journal your child's height, weight, and development with India-appropriate references. Milestone reminders based on the IAP vaccine schedule.

Community — a safe space for Indian parents. Ask, share, and find others at your stage.

Wellness library — curated articles, yoga for pregnancy and postpartum, and recipes tuned to Indian diets (veg, non-veg, eggetarian, vegan).

Government schemes — PMMVY, JSY, state-specific benefits — with eligibility, documents needed, and where to apply.

Multi-child profiles — separate profile for each child, so guidance stays age-appropriate as your family grows.

BUILT IN INDIA, FOR INDIA

MaaMitra is built by an Indian parent who wanted an app that speaks like a real mitra — not a clinical chatbot, not a global app forced onto Indian mothers. Every feature is shaped by what Indian families actually deal with.

A NOTE ON WHAT MAAMITRA IS — AND ISN'T

MaaMitra is a parenting companion, not a medical service. We share information to help you feel informed and less alone, not to diagnose or treat. For anything urgent or specific to your child, please consult a qualified doctor.

PRIVATE BY DEFAULT

• No ads. We don't sell your data.
• Your profile and your baby's information are yours. You can delete your account and all data from inside the app.
• Encrypted in transit. Stored on Google Firebase (same infrastructure that powers Google's own products).

Privacy Policy: https://maamitra.co.in/privacy
Terms: https://maamitra.co.in/terms
Website: https://maamitra.co.in

Questions or feedback? Write to us at hello@maamitra.co.in — we read every one.
```
